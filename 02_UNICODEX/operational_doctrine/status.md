# Operational Status Standards

Status markers are synced 1:1 with the **Notion Global History** database to ensure seamless state persistence across the ecosystem.

## 1. Status Categories

### To-do (Forecast)
- `FORECAST`: Initial ideation or future-dated work.
- `PLANNED`: Validated objective waiting for scheduling.
- `SCHEDULED`: Allocated to a specific timeframe or sprint.
- `CONDITION`: Blocked by or contingent on external triggers.

### In Progress (Active)
- `PENDING`: Unassigned or "In-Prep" (Default for new Missions).
- `ONGOING`: Active execution by an agent or user.
- `SUSPENDED`: Temporarily paused due to friction or priority shift.

### Complete (Closed)
- `DISMISSED`: Objective no longer relevant or superseded.
- `ACQUIRED`: Resource or goal successfully captured.
- `OPERATIONAL`: System or feature is live and functioning.
- `COMPLETED`: Objective fully achieved.
- `ABORTED`: Terminated before completion.
- `FAILED`: Objective was not achieved (Post-mortem required).
