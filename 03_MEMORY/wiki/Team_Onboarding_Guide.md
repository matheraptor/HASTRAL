# Team Onboarding Guide

**Summary**: This guide outlines the onboarding process, required standards, and resources for new personnel joining the HASTRAL enterprise.

## Onboarding Philosophy

Onboarding at HASTRAL is not merely a procedural checklist but a strategic integration process. As stated by [[@Organizational_Structure#Valerie_Vane|Valerie Vane]], "Onboarding is not complete until synergy is established."

The process is designed to align new personnel with the [[@HR_Policies_Framework#Ethical_Kernel|Immutable Ethical Kernel]] and ensure mastery of the system's deterministic protocols.

## The Onboarding Process

### 1. Initial Profile Generation
New personnel profiles are generated using the **GEM v1.2** framework, focusing on psychometric auditing and strategic role placement.

### 2. The Confirmation Gate (§4)
Every new hire must pass through the **Confirmation Gate**. This involves a series of validation audits to ensure technical competence and ethical alignment. No personnel can advance to active duty without explicit validation.

### 3. Identity Resolution
Finalizing a staff member's "Sovereign Identity." This involves moving beyond base persona traits to a fixed, deterministic role within the enterprise (e.g., transition from "Curator" to "Lexington Thorne, Architect").

### 4. Technical Mastering
New members must demonstrate proficiency in:
- **Markdown §9 Syntax**: The mandatory standard for all documentation.
- **MEMA Scaling**: Understanding the MAGPIE Ecosystem Memory & Adaptation scales (0-4).
- **Deterministic Protocols**: Operating within a zero-trust architecture.

## Resources and Mentorship

### Documentation Standards
All staff are required to maintain clarity and avoid jargon without explanation. The **Lexicographical Drive** ensures that the knowledge base remains an anti-entropy filter for the entire team.

### Peer Mentorship
New members are often paired with "System-Siblings" or established leads to facilitate integration:
- **Recruitment Guidance**: Valerie Vane.
- **Narrative Coordination**: Lexington Thorne.
- **Compliance Validation**: Aurum Krell.
- **Operational Support**: Mara Vane.

## Success Criteria

A successful onboarding concludes when:
- The personnel profile is standardized to the **Elvin Standard**.
- The member has passed their "First Day Audit."
- Synergy with the assigned team (e.g., Team Raptor, Vane Triad) is established.
