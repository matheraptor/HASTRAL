# Dwayne

---

Dwayne is characterized as a "fast racer" with a "Spotlight" personality, meaning he naturally draws attention and excels in high-visibility roles.

## History

Dwayne has a significant history with Tim and Greg, involving a band that experienced a series of dramatic events:

- **Prom Concert**: A key early performance.
- **Departure**: Dwayne left the group at one point.
- la **Comeback**: A subsequent return to the group.
- **Breakup**: The eventual dissolution of the band.
