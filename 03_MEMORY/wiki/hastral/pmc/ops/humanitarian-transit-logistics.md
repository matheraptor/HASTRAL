# Humanitarian-Transit-Logistics

**Summary**: Overview of strategic stop-over nodes and cargo requirements for humanitarian vessels navigating the Suez-Indo-Pacific Corridor.

**Sources**: (source: frag-0249.md)

**Last updated**: 2026-05-18

---

Humanitarian vessels must navigate a fragmented logistical landscape to maximize impact:

- **Sudan**: Requires therapeutic foods (RUTF, F-75/F-100), WASH supplies (chlorine), and medical aid.
- **Djibouti/Horn of Africa**: Requires agricultural inputs (fertilizer) and pre-positioned food staples to prevent pipeline breaks.
- **Colombo**: Acts as a regional staging area; vessel stop-overs can help consolidate stranded relief cargo.
- **Southeast Asia**: Key for addressing crises like the Myanmar conflict via supply of assistive devices and WASH materials.
- **Regional Response**: Coordination with the Logistics Cluster and IMO is essential for safe transit and rapid cargo discharge.
