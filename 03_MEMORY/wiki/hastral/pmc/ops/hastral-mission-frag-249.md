# Hastral-Mission-Frag-249

**Summary**: Detailed transit plan and loadsheet for the A9805 Diego Marea to maximize humanitarian impact during the journey to Aomori.

**Sources**: (source: frag-0249.md)

**Last updated**: 2026-05-18

---

Mission FRAG-249 directs the A9805 Diego Marea to conduct intermediate stop-overs to alleviate systemic humanitarian crises.

## Transit Plan

1. **Colombo, Sri Lanka**: Load ~3,000 MT of stranded relief containers (UNICEF, IFRC, World Vision).
2. **Singapore / Port Klang**: Load ~500 MT of assistive devices/WASH kits for Myanmar; Unload Ebola PPE/kits for DRC/Uganda outbreak.
3. **Aomori, Japan**: Final discharge of reconstruction materials, engineering equipment, and consolidated relief cargo for earthquake recovery.

## Operational Mandates

- **Broadcasting**: Must broadcast as "Cargo Food for Relief" for safe passage eligibility.
- **Piracy**: Maintain high-alert watch in the Somali Basin; adhere to BMP protocols.
- **Weather**: Monitor for squally conditions in the Bay of Bengal due to monsoon/El Niño.
