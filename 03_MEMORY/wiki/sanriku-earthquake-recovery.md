# Page Title: Sanriku Earthquake Recovery

**Summary**: Overview of the Sanriku seismic event (April 2026) and humanitarian requirements for the reconstruction of Aomori and the Tohoku region.

**Sources**: [[frag-0249-aomori.md]]

**Last updated**: 2026-05-24

---

The vessel's final destination, Aomori, Japan, is a zone of active disaster recovery following an M7.7 earthquake on April 20, 2026 (source: frag-0249-aomori.md).

### Recovery Requirements

Essential commodities for the "Reconstruction and Beyond 2026" initiative include (source: frag-0249-aomori.md):

- **Shelter/NFIs**: Priority for non-food items and shelter.
- **Medical/Psychosocial**: Dedicated support for displaced elderly populations.
- **Engineering/Steel**: Structural steel and engineering equipment for reconstruction.
