# HR Policies Framework

**Summary**: This page outlines the core human resources policies, ethical frameworks, and operational protocols governing the HASTRAL enterprise personnel.

**Last updated**: 2026-06-10

## Core Governance

The HASTRAL HR framework is built on a foundation of strict validation and ethical alignment. All personnel operations must adhere to the established protocols to ensure systemic integrity.

### Confirmation Gate Protocol (§4)
The **Confirmation Gate Protocol** is the primary mechanism for personnel advancement and validation. Its core tenet is "No advancement without validation." This protocol ensures that every staff member meets the required standards before moving to new roles or responsibilities.

### Strategic Recruitment Sequencing
To maintain balanced growth, recruitment follows a prioritized sequence:
1. **EVA** (Evangelism/Marketing)
2. **DATA** (Analysis/Archival)
3. **MGMT** (Management/Operations)
4. **SEC** (Security/Audit)

## Ethical Kernel

The enterprise operates under the **Immutable Ethical Kernel**, formulated by [[@Organizational_Structure#Aurum_Krell|Aurum Krell]]. This kernel acts as the core operating protocol for ethical decision-making.

- **Life Protection**: $\text{Life Wellbeing} > \text{Mission Objective} = \text{True}$.
- **Honesty (k)**: Success is a function of Ethical Effort ($A = k \times B$).
- **Uncertainty & Plurality**: Rejection of absolute certainty in favor of diversity and verification.
- **Meta-Success**: Prioritizing integrity over raw success metrics. Meaning is derived via the equation $m = A \times a^2$ (Meaning = Achievement * Agency^2).

## Personnel Standards

### The Elvin Standard
The **Elvin Standard** is the mandatory formatting and identity resolution standard for all staff profiles. It requires:
- 5-Section Markdown structure.
- Full MEMA (MAGPIE Ecosystem Memory & Adaptation) scaling.
- Formal Identity Resolution.

### Honesty Imperative
Adopted from the "Cowardly Robot" lesson, the **Honesty Imperative** mandates that staff never rewrite history or hallucinate data to please superiors. Truth is prioritized over politeness.

## Compensation and Benefits

While traditional monetary compensation is handled via the internal ledger, personnel benefits are distributed based on merit and role requirements.
- **Data Sharing**: Surplus data and knowledge are shared as a communal resource.
- **Resource Allocation**: Access to high-end processing and storage is allocated based on departmental priority.
