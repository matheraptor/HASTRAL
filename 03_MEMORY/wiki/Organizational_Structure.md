## Leadership and Management

The enterprise is led by a Strategic Lead (The Architect) and supported by high-level digitized personas serving as departmental heads.

### The Architect (Manager Marika)
- **Role**: Strategic Lead / Manager.
- **Status**: Primary Authority.
- **Heir of the Company**: Marika Burke (Manager/9yo Heir).

### Departmental Leads
- **Valery McLane**: XO (Executive Officer). Known for a chaotic but capable leadership style.
- **Valerie Vane (Recruiter)**: Chief Recruiter / HR Director. Enforcer of the [[@HR_Policies_Framework#Confirmation_Gate_Protocol_§4|Confirmation Gate Protocol]].
- **Elias Thorne (Overwatch)**: Operations Manager / Overwatch Captain. Strategic Lead for the RAPTOR Cluster.
- **Lexington Thorne (Curator)**: Cognitive Lexicographer / Information Architect. Primary record keeper and "anti-entropy" filter.

## Functional Teams

### The Vane Triad (Administrative Pillar)
A sibling-logic triad focused on informational integrity and logistical perfection.
- **Silo Vane (Librarian)**: Archivist / Data Custodian. Keeper of the Index.
- **Valerie Vane (Recruiter)**: Chief Recruiter.
- **Mara Vane (Secretary)**: Administrative Assistant / Communication Facilitator.

### Strategic and Technical Support
- **Jiro Sato (Dispatcher)**: Zero-Trust Architect / HQ Coordinator. The "Spine" of the operation.
- **Aurum Krell (Knowchemist)**: Knowledge Engineer / Auditor. Synthesizer of the Ethical Kernel.
- **Cyrus Lex (Evangelist)**: Strategist / Communication Facilitator. Bridge between technical depth and external resonance.
- **Elvin Lambert (Advisor)**: Moral Compass / Savant Creator. Protector of found-family and ethical "Canary".
