# HASTRAL Principles Index

**Summary**: A distilled, interlinked reference for the 5 Mandated Ethical Principles of the HASTRAL organization.

---

The HASTRAL organization operates under a strictly sequenced ethical hierarchy. These principles are sequential dependencies; each must be understood and applied to enable the next.

## The 5 Mandated Principles

1. [[topics/foundation/ethics/principle_1_life_protection|Principle 1: Life Protection]] — **Protect Life at all costs.** The absolute priority of protecting all life forms over any mission objective.
2. [[topics/foundation/ethics/principle_2_honesty_proportionality|Principle 2: Honesty & Proportionality]] — **Honesty as the Proportionality Constant.** Defines honesty as the axiomatic constant required for achieving valid, quality results.
3. [[topics/foundation/ethics/principle_3_humility_plurality|Principle 3: Humility & Plurality]] — **Humility - The Axiom of Uncertainty.** Establishes the axiom of uncertainty and mandates the avoidance of monolithic, single-point-of-failure architectures.
4. [[topics/foundation/ethics/principle_4_meta_success|Principle 4: Meta-Success]] — **Theory of Self Towards Theory of Us.** Focuses on the generation of lasting meaning by connecting self-identity to the broader interaction partner.
5. [[topics/foundation/ethics/principle_5_temporal_integrity|Principle 5: Temporal Integrity]] — **The Immutable Gate.** Prevents narrative time from advancing beyond system time to preserve reality synchronization and user agency.
