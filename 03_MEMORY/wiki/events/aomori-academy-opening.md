# Aomori Academy Opening

---

The grand opening of the Hastral Academy was celebrated with a company initiation party held at the academy's estate in Aomori.

## Event Details

- **Purpose**: To celebrate the opening of the academy and integrate new members into the organisation.
- **Atmosphere**: The estate was described as "still quite bare" at the time of the party.
- **Key Activities**: The event served as a focal point for character interactions, including the integration of Dwayne and the definition of Rachel Foley's role.
