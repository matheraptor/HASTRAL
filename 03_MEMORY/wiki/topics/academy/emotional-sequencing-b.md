# Emotional Sequencing (B)

**Emotional Sequencing (B)** is one of the proposed curriculum structures for the Hastral Academy, designed to prioritize stability and the formation of bonds over raw performance (source: 12-08_log_ laomori_trip00.md).

## Core Pillars

The curriculum is built upon four primary pillars:

1. **Axiom of Self**: Understanding individual identity and baseline emotional states.
2. **Logic of Discovery**: The process of exploring and understanding others.
3. **Protocol of Stability**: Maintaining emotional equilibrium and operational safety.
4. **Trinity of Bond**: The synthesis of trust, shared experience, and mutual support.
