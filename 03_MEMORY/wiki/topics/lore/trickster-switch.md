# Trickster Switch

**Summary**: A musical project and band of misfits who use a "Dinosaur Approach" to achieve viral success and rediscover their bond through music.
**Sources**: 6-09_lore_TricksterSwitch.md
**Last updated**: 2026-06-12

**Trickster Switch** is a band characterized by its eclectic membership and a unique marketing strategy involving dressing as dinosaurs (source: 6-09_lore_TricksterSwitch.md).

## Narrative Arc

The band's history is a journey of friendship, failure, and redemption:

- **Origins**: Formed by Tim and Elvin, who bonded over a shared love of dinosaurs and metal music.
- **The Grind**: Achieved local success but struggled to secure a record deal.
- **The Breakup**: The band fractured due to personal conflicts, drug abuse (Rachel), and legal issues (Pawn).
- **The Comeback**: Reunited with a new lead singer, Moni (Tim's daughter), and culminated in a final, perfect tribute show.

### Key Members

- **Tim**: Drummer and emotional core.
- **Elvin**: Bassist and moral compass.
- **Greg**: Singer and pianist.
- **Rachel**: Guitarist.
- **Pawn**: Rhythm guitarist.
- **Moni**: Lead singer (comeback era).

### The "Dinosaur Approach"

To market their final show, the band dressed as dinosaurs, which led to a "nuclear" success as audiences were drawn by curiosity and the spectacle.
