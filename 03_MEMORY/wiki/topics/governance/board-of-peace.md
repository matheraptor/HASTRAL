# Board of Peace (BoP)

**Summary**: An oversight body that monitors and suppresses unauthorized AI and compute signatures using advanced surveillance hardware.
**Sources**: 5-.hastral-raptor.md
**Last updated**: 2026-06-12

The **Board of Peace (BoP)** is a regulatory and oversight entity that enforces strict controls on AI deployment and compute signatures (source: 5-.hastral-raptor.md).

## Surveillance Capabilitie

- **Argus-7 "High-inference Sniffers"**: Specialized hardware designed to detect "Compute Signatures" and suppress unauthorized AI. These sniffers were used in Manila to physically block access to humanitarian cargo on the [[L9706 Hestia Cradle]] to force a technical hold.

### Conflict with Hastral

The BoP's interference in Manila damaged Hastral's reputation as a "Good Guy" operator and forced the organization to implement the "Air-Bridge" solution to bypass BoP technical holds.
