# Triple Emergency (May 2026)

The "Triple Emergency" describes the convergence of three major crises that severely impacted global maritime logistics and humanitarian aid supply chains in May 2026 (source: 1-.hastral-ops-0249.md).

- **Strait of Hormuz Closure**: De facto closure impacting energy and aid flows.
- **Somali Piracy**: Resurgence of Pirate Action Groups (PAGs) in the Somali Basin.
- **Sanriku Earthquake**: Devastating seismic activity in Northern Japan, creating urgent humanitarian needs in Aomori.

These events necessitated the establishment of "Safe Corridors" and the use of the SAGAR doctrine to ensure the transit of critical relief cargo.
