# Task Force Raptor

**Summary**: A specialized Hastral maritime task force used for strategic deployment, humanitarian cover, and the covert staging of Project ADELE.
**Sources**: 5-.hastral-raptor.md
**Last updated**: 2026-06-12

**Task Force Raptor** is a high-capability maritime unit that employs a "Humanitarian Trojan Horse" strategy to mask strategic operations under the guise of WFP-aligned logistical support (source: 5-.hastral-raptor.md).

## Operational History

- **Cape Town (2025-2026)**: Secured priority berthing at Berth F-4 by reclassifying fuel as "Essential Support Infrastructure."
- **Indian Ocean**: Utilized Port Louis, Mauritius, as an endurance anchor for bunkering.
- **Jakarta**: Staged for the South China Sea corridor.
- **Tokyo**: Conducted the "Akihabara Pilgrimage" as a PR leak operation.
- **Manila**: Faced a crisis where the Board of Peace (BoP) used Argus-7 sniffers to block humanitarian grain, necessitating an "Air-Bridge" solution.
- **Okinawa**: Current staging point, focusing on Smart Grid restoration and civil protection.

### Tactical Assets

- **Vessels**: [[L9706 Hestia Cradle]], [[C38 Floriana Marea]], [[A9805 Diego Marea]], and the ROKN DDG-995 (used for visual distraction).
- **Aviation**: [[F-GZMK (Falcon 8X)]] and S-70 Seahawk "Fred".
