# RoboUno Protocol

---

RoboUno is a modified version of the card game Uno, repurposed as a communication protocol. Instead of matching numbers or colors for the sake of winning, players use "colored moves" to signal their intent and emotional state during a conversation.

## Move Types

- **BLUE**: Represents stability, clarity, agreement, or a defensive posture. Used to stabilize a situation or request clear information.
- **GREEN**: Represents growth, action, progress, or a positive shift in dynamics.
- **YELLOW**: Represents curiosity, questioning, and the exploration of new ideas.
- **RED**: Represents challenge, conflict, confrontation, or a "read" on the other person's state.

## Mechanics

- **Card Matching**: Players can match values or colors to maintain the flow of the interaction.
- **Hand Privacy**: Players' hands are generally private, though access can be granted to a "Master" (director) for oversight.
- **Purpose**: The goal is not to "win" in the traditional sense, but to reach a state of mutual understanding or a resolved decision.
