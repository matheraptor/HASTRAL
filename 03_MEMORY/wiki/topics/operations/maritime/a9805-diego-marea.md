## Specifications
- **Class**: Mars-class Combat Stores Ship.
- **Capacity**: High-tonnage cargo capability; noted as having ~18,000 tons of underutilized capacity during the May 2026 transit.

## Operational History
- **Recent Deployment**: Completed Mission SPERANZA II in the Gaza theater.
- **Current Assignment**: Executing [[mission-frag-040]], transiting the Arabian Sea toward Aomori, Japan.

## Role in HASTRAL
The A9805 serves as a primary logistical artery for the delivery of humanitarian aid in contested or high-risk maritime environments, operating under the [[sagar-doctrine]] for safe corridor transit.
