# SAGAR Doctrine

The SAGAR (Security and Growth for All in the Region) doctrine provides the diplomatic and operational framework for ensuring the safe passage of humanitarian cargo through contested or high-risk waters.

## Operational Application

Under the SAGAR doctrine, vessels such as the A9805 Diego Marea broadcast their status as "Cargo Food for Relief" to signal non-combatant status and maintain eligibility for safe corridors, particularly when interacting with regional vetting systems like those of the IRGC.
