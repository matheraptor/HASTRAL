# Sudan Famine Crisis (2026)

The conflict in Sudan has escalated into a catastrophic humanitarian and protection crisis, characterized by widespread famine and systemic violence.

## Crisis Dimensions

- **Food Insecurity**: Nearly 19.5 million people face acute food shortages.
- **Systemic Violence**: Reports of mass killings, summary executions, and ethnically-motivated violence.
- **Protection Risks**: Rampant conflict-related sexual violence and the recruitment/abduction of children.
- **Infrastructure Collapse**: Systematic attacks on hospitals, schools, and markets.

## Displacement and Needs

- **Internal Displacement**: Approximately 9.6 million people remain internally displaced, living in precarious conditions.
- **Urgent Requirements**: There is a critical need to scale up protection, mental health, and psychosocial support services due to widespread trauma.
