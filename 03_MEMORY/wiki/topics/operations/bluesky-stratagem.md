# Bluesky Stratagem

**Summary**: A PR operation utilizing the @matheraptor account to frame strategic offshore transfers as "heroic saves" to bypass corporate filters.
**Sources**: 5-.hastral-raptor.md
**Last updated**: 2026-06-12

The **Bluesky Stratagem** is a narrative control operation designed to maintain PR dominance and bypass corporate filters during logistical failures (source: 5-.hastral-raptor.md).

## Execution

During the Manila crisis, when the Board of Peace (BoP) blocked grain delivery, Hastral used the `@matheraptor` account to target gamedev and writing communities. By framing the situation as a "heroic save," they successfully shifted the public narrative and bypassed corporate filters.
