# Italian Decree Law 159/2025

Decree Law 159/2025 introduces strict regulatory requirements for Third Sector entities involved in civil protection and humanitarian operations within Italy.

## Key Provisions

- **Volunteer Equivalence**: Volunteers are now legally equated to workers regarding their rights and obligations for training, Personal Protective Equipment (PPE), and health monitoring.
- **Health and Safety Obligations**: Organizations must implement rigorous health and safety protocols to protect personnel in high-risk environments.
- **Training Requirements**: Mandatory training must be provided based on specific risk scenarios associated with the deployment.
- **Sanitary Control**: Maintenance of a "control sanitary" (health record) is required for all personnel.

## Operational Impact

These regulations ensure a professional standard of care and safety for volunteers, reducing the liability of the organization and increasing the resilience of the humanitarian workforce.
