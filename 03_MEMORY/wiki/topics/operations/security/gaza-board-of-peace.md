# Gaza Board of Peace

The "Board of Peace" is a US-backed strategic plan designed to stabilize the Gaza theater through the introduction of an international security architecture.

## The International Stabilization Force (ISF)

- **Leadership**: Led by Major General Jasper Jeffers.
- **Composition**: Includes significant contributions from international partners, such as Indonesia, which has committed up to 8,000 troops.
- **Role**: Tasked with maintaining security and facilitating humanitarian access.

## Operational Risks

- **Overlapping Control**: There is a high risk of "overlapping areas of control" involving the IDF, Hamas, local militias, and private security contractors (e.g., UG Solutions).
- **Power Vacuums**: The control of key crossings, such as Rafah, by local militias creates power vacuums that complicate humanitarian access and security.
