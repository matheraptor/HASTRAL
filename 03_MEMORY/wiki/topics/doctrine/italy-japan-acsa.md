# Italy-Japan ACSA

The Acquisition and Cross-Service Agreement (ACSA) is a bilateral framework designed to strengthen defense cooperation and facilitate the exchange of military supplies between Italy and Japan.

## Scope and Objectives

- **Humanitarian Mobilization**: The agreement specifically targets the mobilization of aid during humanitarian crises and natural disasters.
- **Trade Route Protection**: It facilitates cooperation in the protection of international trade routes.
- **Joint Innovation**: Promotes collaborative development through programs such as G-CAP.

## Operational Impact

The ACSA allows for more efficient logistics and supply chain management when deploying assets for joint relief operations, such as those seen in the aftermath of the Sanriku Earthquake.
