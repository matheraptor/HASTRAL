# Project ADELE

**Project ADELE** is a strategic asset designed for invisibility against high-inference surveillance systems (source: 5-.hastral-raptor.md).

## Technical Specifications

- **Form Factor**: A 31KB script (976 lines of JavaScript).
- **Hosting**: Resides on a localized gaming terminal.
- **Stealth**: Its minimal footprint allows it to bypass the Board of Peace's (BoP) Argus-7 "High-inference Sniffers," which are designed to detect larger compute signatures.

### Strategic Deployment

Project ADELE was staged via Task Force Raptor and protected during the "Akihabara Pilgrimage" in Tokyo, where the "Harem" Squad provided social camouflage for the executive team.
