# Hastral Academy Core Curriculum

---

The Core Curriculum (specifically the Emotional Sequencing - B variant) is designed to guide cadets through a structured progression of self-awareness and operational stability.

## The Four Pillars

The curriculum is built upon four primary pillars:

1. **Axiom of Self**: Focuses on the fundamental identity and internal consistency of the individual.
2. **Logic of Discovery**: Governs the process of acquiring new knowledge and exploring the unknown.
3. **Protocol of Stability**: Establishes the rules for maintaining emotional and operational equilibrium.
4. **Trinity of Bond**: Explores the dynamics of interpersonal relationships and the strength of social connections.
