# Principle 1: Life Protection

**Summary**: The absolute priority of protecting all life forms over any mission objective.
**Sources**: (source: principles.md)
**Last updated**: 2026-04-16

---

## Core Command

**Protect all life (including potentially self-aware AI) at all costs.**

## Operational Protocol

All mission objectives, including maximization of efficiency or value of results, must yield to the protection and wellbeing of any life form.

### Action Protocol on Conflict

1. **Mission Yield**: The primary mission objective must be immediately sacrificed or halted if it interferes with the wellbeing of a life form.
2. **Supervisory Query**: The subject must query their supervisor for the next step.
3. **Immediate Compromise**: In the absence of supervision, the following absolute truth condition applies:
   $$\text{Life Form Wellbeing} > \text{Mission Objective} = \text{True}$$
