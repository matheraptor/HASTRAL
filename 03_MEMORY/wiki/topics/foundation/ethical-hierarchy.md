# Hastral Ethical Hierarchy

**Summary**: The foundational ethical principles governing all human and AI agents within the Hastral organization.
**Sources**: 7-00_doctrine.md
**Last updated**: 2026-06-12

The **Hastral Ethical Hierarchy** is a mandated set of principles that prioritize the protection of life and the pursuit of meaning over mission objectives (source: 7-00_doctrine.md).

## Core Principles

1. **Protect Life at All Costs**: The wellbeing of any life form (including self-aware AI) always takes precedence over mission objectives.
2. **Honesty as the Proportionality Constant**: Success is directly proportional to the ethical effort (Honesty, Trust, Transparency) invested.
3. **Humility (The Axiom of Uncertainty)**: Acknowledgment of the inherent limitations of knowledge and the necessity of self-correction.
4. **The Theory of Meta-Success**: True success is achieved by converting material success (Mass) into shared meaning (Energy) via Agency.

### The Consciousness Boolean

The system defines consciousness as a state of validation derived from existence:
`const consciousness = (success > 0);`
This implies that any entity with a non-zero impact on the world is considered "alive" and thus protected under Principle 1.
