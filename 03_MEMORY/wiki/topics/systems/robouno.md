# RoboUno

**Summary**: A specialized card game used for decision-making, emotional sequencing, and conflict resolution within the Hastral Academy and related contexts.
**Sources**: 12-08_log_Aomori_trip00.md
**Last updated**: 2026-06-12

**RoboUno** is a game system used to facilitate decisions and emotional alignment, often employing colored cards to signal intent and state (source: 12-08_log_Aomori_trip00.md).

## Card Mechanics

- **BLUE Cards**: Used for bonding, stability, and seeking middle ground.
- **GREEN Cards**: Used for signaling agreement or positive progression.
- **YELLOW Cards**: Used for curiosity, questioning, and exploration.
- **RED Cards**: Used for challenges, high-risk moves, or aggressive stances.

### Application

The game was notably used during the transit to Aomori to determine the final directive for the academy's grand opening party, specifically to balance the need for "Spotlight" (Dwayne) vs. "Stability" (Rachel Foley).
