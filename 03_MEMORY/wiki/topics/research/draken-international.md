# Draken International

**Draken International** is a private military company founded by Jared Isaacman that provides "adversary support" to national air forces (source: 3-07_Intel.md).

## Operational Mode

Draken acts as a "red team," simulating realistic enemy squadrons to train military pilots. This model provides cost savings for governments and enhances training by providing a perspective that internal military doctrines cannot replicate.

### Fleet Capabilities

Draken operates one of the largest privately owned tactical airwings in the world, with over 150 aircraft, including:

- **A-4 Skyhawk**
- **Mirage F1**
- **MiG-21**
- **F-16 Fighting Falcon**

The company's airpower is estimated to be superior to that of many sovereign states, yet it remains strictly dedicated to a non-combat, support-oriented mission.
