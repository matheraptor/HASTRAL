# Liberland

**Liberland** is a micronation that claims territory in an area unclaimed by both Croatia and Serbia along the Danube river (source: 3-07_Intel.md). Despite its land area being significantly larger than the Vatican, it has not received international recognition and is dismissed by neighboring nations as "frivolous."
