# Statehood & Sovereignty

The process of creating a recognized country is primarily a matter of international agreement and recognition (source: 3-07_Intel.md).

## Requirements for Statehood

1. **Land Claim**: Establishing a territory, either through claiming unclaimed land (e.g., [[Liberland]]) or splitting from an existing state.
2. **Permanent Population**: A sustainable population that resides in the territory.
3. **Government**: A functioning system of laws and principles, typically based on a constitution.
4. **International Recognition**: The most difficult step, culminating in admission to the United Nations (UN).
