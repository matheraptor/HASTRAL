# Mission FRAG-040

**Summary**: A humanitarian transit and relief logistics mission involving the A9805 Diego Marea from Limassol to Aomori.
**Sources**: 1-.hastral-ops-0249.md
**Last updated**: 2026-06-1

Mission FRAG-040 focuses on the strategic transit of the [[A9805 Diego Marea]] (Mars-class Combat Stores Ship) to deliver humanitarian aid to Aomori, Japan, following the Sanriku Earthquake (source: 1-.hastral-ops-0249.md).

## Transit Route & Stop-overs

The vessel transits the [[Suez-Indo-Pacific Corridor]] with planned intermediate stops to maximize humanitarian impact:

1. **Port of Colombo, Sri Lanka**: Loading stranded relief containers (UNICEF, IFRC, World Vision) to address a backlog of ~300 containers.
2. **Port of Singapore / Port Klang, Malaysia**: Loading assistive devices and WASH materials for survivors of the 2025 Myanmar earthquake and conflict-displaced populations.

### Operational Constraints

- **Security**: High-alert watch for Somali Pirate Action Groups (PAGs) and adherence to BMP protocols.
- **Meteorology**: Navigation during the onset of the Southwest Monsoon (Kerala onset May 26).
- **Protocol**: Broadcasting status as "Cargo Food for Relief" to maintain "Safe Corridor" eligibility under the SAGAR doctrine.
