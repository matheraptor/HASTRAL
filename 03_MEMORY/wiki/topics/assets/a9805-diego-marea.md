# A9805 Diego Marea

The **A9805 Diego Marea** is a Mars-class Combat Stores Ship (source: 1-.hastral-ops-0249.md).

In May 2026, it was tasked with [[Mission FRAG-040]], transiting from Limassol to Aomori. During this transit, the vessel had approximately 18,000 tons of underutilized capacity, which was leveraged to transport stranded relief containers from Colombo and WASH materials from Singapore/Klang.
