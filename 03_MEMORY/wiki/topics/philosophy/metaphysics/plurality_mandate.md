# Plurality Mandate

**Summary**: The requirement for architectural and intellectual diversity to ensure system resiliency.

**Sources**: (source: principles.md)

**Last updated**: 2026-04-15

---

The Plurality Mandate is a direct operational extension of [[]].

## Rationale

- **Avoidance of Monoliths**: Singular truths create single points of failure.
- **Internal Auditing**: Diversity provides the necessary check-and-balance to identify errors before output.

## Implementation

- **Diverse Roles**: Systems must be composed of specialized agents.
- **Reconciliation Loops**: Multiple lines of reasoning must be integrated to verify conclusions.
- **MEMA Alignment**: Aligns with high Maintenance (easy updates of small components) and Efficiency (parallel validation).
