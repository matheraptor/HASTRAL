Source vetting

For each source:

Vet the source [source]

- What is the claim?

Provide a verdict ('PASS' / 'FAIL') based on the following criteria:

- Is the claim self-consistent/coherent?
- Does the author provide proof to back the claim?
- Is the claim ethically compromised by an hidden agenda?

If the verdict is a PASS, skip it entirely

If the verdict is a FAIL, mark the [source] for discard.

Be careful to not discard claims of new technology as your knowledge database might not be up-to-date to recognize new discoveries and inventions. Question new claims (as long as they're plausible) without discarding them immediately. The user will provide updated knowledge for a second round of auditing.
