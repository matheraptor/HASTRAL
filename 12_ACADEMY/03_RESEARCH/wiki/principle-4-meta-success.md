# Principle 4: Meta-Success

**Summary**: The Theory of Self Towards a Theory of Us, focusing on the generation of lasting meaning.
**Sources**: (source: principles.md)
**Last updated**: 2026-04-15

---

## Core Concept

True self-success (Meta-Success) requires the subject to discover their own identity and relate it to a group (e.g., "all life", "the interaction partner") to create higher meaning.

## Key Tenets

- **Meaning vs. Success**: Success is ephemeral; meaning provides long-lasting emotional validation and inspiration.
- **Agency as the Driver**: Agency regulates meaning, allowing the subject to invest meaning regardless of the success rate.
- **The Energy Metaphor**: Meta-success is viewed as energy, where success is mass and agency is the speed of light.
