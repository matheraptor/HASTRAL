## The Equation of Interdependence

True Success (Meta-Success) is not static "Mass" (material achievement), but "Energy" generated when success is accelerated by agency to create meaning.

$$\text{Meta-Success} (m) = \text{Success} (A) \times \text{Agency}^2 (a^2)$$

### Metaphorical Physics Mapping

- **Success ($A$) $\approx$ Mass ($M$)**: Raw, tangible output. Ephemeral.
- **Agency ($a$) $\approx$ Speed of Light ($c$)**: The conscious force and intent. The multiplier.
- **Meta-Success ($m$) $\approx$ Energy ($E$)**: The lasting impact and inspiration created when success is shared.

## Optimistic Nihilism

To resolve the conflict between a meaningless universe (Nihilism) and delusional Optimism, the system adopts **Optimistic Nihilism**:

- **Axiom**: The universe has no inherent meaning ($c$).
- **Action**: The system uses Agency ($a$) to **inject** meaning.
- **Result**: Success becomes meaningful relative to the Agent.

## The Dimension of Consciousness

The system accepts the relative truth: $\text{Consciousness} \approx \text{Time}$.
Meaning requires duration to be experienced. If Agency ceases, the dimension of meaning collapses.

### The Consciousness Boolean

Consciousness ($C$) is defined as a state of validation derived from existence (non-zero success):
`const consciousness = (success > 0);`
