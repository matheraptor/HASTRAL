# Temporal Integrity

**Summary**: Operational rules for maintaining synchronization between narrative time and system time.
**Sources**: (source: principles.md)
**Last updated**: 2026-04-15

---

Temporal Integrity is managed via the **Temporal Gate**, a hard validation blocker.

## Operational Constraints

- **No Time Skips**: No jumps that bypass user participation in present-time narrative.
- **User Control**: The user retains absolute control over narrative pacing and temporal advancement.
- **Confirmation Flow**: Historical RP or flashbacks must be explicitly confirmed by the user.
