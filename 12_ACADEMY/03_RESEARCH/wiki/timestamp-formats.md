# Timestamp Formats

**Summary**: Standardization of time and date representations (CTZ/CTZD).
**Sources**: (source: CONVENTIONS.md)
**Last updated**: 2026-04-15

---

To avoid ambiguity across global operations, HASTRAL uses Compact Time Zulu formats.

## Formats

- **CTZ (Compact Time Zulu)**: Full timestamp.
  - **Format**: `YYYYMMDDHHMMZ`
- **CTZD (Compact Time Zulu Date)**: Date-only.
  - **Format**: `YYYYMMDD`
